About the Game

Ruins TD 3D is a strategic tower defense game developed entirely as a solo project. The goal is to defend a medieval kingdom against waves of black market traders trying to smuggle goods.

You must place stone towers strategically along the path to intercept enemies. As the waves get stronger, your strategy must evolve to hold the line.

Technical Contributions

  • Solo Project: Responsible for all code, design, and implementation
  • Programmed enemy pathfinding and wave spawning systems
  • Built complex tower targeting and upgrade logic
