About the Game

WA-LCH is a 3D top-down adventure game where you step into the tracks of a cleaning bot named LCH. Set in a dark underground laboratory, your mission is to restore power by reactivating generators.

But it's not just about cleaning up; you must defend yourself and the facility from mysterious threats lurking in the shadows.

Technical Contributions

  • Programmed helper robot logic using NavMesh and timers
  • Designed levels and generator interaction mechanics
  • Implemented shooting and damage systems for combat reliability
